Playing career

Kylan mbappe has so far played for these following clubs

  1. Started his career at the academy of As monaco
  2. 2017, Mbappe at 16 years old was called up to the first team of As monaco where he recieved his first proffesional expreience
  3. After balling out in the world cup, and impressing for As monaco he was signed to Paris-saint-german aged 19 years old in 2019
  4. In the year 2024, Mbappe signed a long term contract at Real Madrid

Skills and attributes

Mbappe exhibits world class physical and technical ability. He specializes in getting out of tight spaces, and using his rapid pace to beat defenders. His pace is definetly his best attribute, He broke the world record for fastest speed recorded in soccer history with a blistering pace of 38 km/hr. He also is a really good finisher of the ball
